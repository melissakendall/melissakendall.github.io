Welcome

Melissa Kendall - Business Analyst, Techical Writer. Melissa lives in Regina, Saskatchewan; the capital city that feels like a small town. After graduating from the University of Manitoba with B.Sc. in Computer Science, Melissa found a way to combine her two passions, writing and programming, and became a Technical Writer. Alas, the allure of complex projects and organizational challenges lured her away and she currently works as Solution Analyst for iQmetrix. Melissa is a passionate public speaker, board game enthusiast and organizer of local community events aimed at getting kids interested in programming.
