20 minutes preparation, 30 minutes cooking time (Excluding cooking time)

**Ingredients**

* 4 cups cooked white rice, cooled
* ¾ cup grated Parmesean
* 1 tsp oregano
* ½ tsp salt
* ¼ tsp black pepper
* 4 eggs
* 8 small cubes mozzarella
* 1 seasoned breadcrumbs

**Directions**

1. Preheat oven to 450 F and line a large pan with tin foil, spray with nonstick cooking spray
2. Place one bowl with bread crumbs and one with two remaining eggs, whisked, next to your preparation station.
3. Combine rice, parmsean, two eggs, oregano, salt and pepper in a large bowl with your hands.
4. Form each aranchini by taking a piece of mozzarella and forming a ball around it with rice, dipping in egg then rolling in breadcrumbs. Place on pan
5. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown

**Notes**

*PRO TIP*: Choice of rice is important but not critical. Classic aranchini is made with arborio rice, sushi rice works well too. If using normal long grain rice, add slightly more water when cooking to create a stickier rice, or add a small amount of rice vinegar.

*ALTERNATIVE*: Cook rice in chicken stock for a more flavourful ball

*ALTERNATIVE*: Add anything you want to the balls! Sausage, ham, bacon, etc.
