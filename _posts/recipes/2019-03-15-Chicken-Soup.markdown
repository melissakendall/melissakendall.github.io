**Description**

20 minutes preparation time, 40 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

* 1 store-bought roast chicken
* 3 L chicken broth
* 5 large carrots cut into rounds or half rounds
* (Optional) 2 large stalks celery, sliced
* 1 tbs Chicken Noodle Soup Seasoning
* 1 package egg noodles

**Directions**

1.Separate chicken meat from bones, reserve meat
2. Bring to a boil chicken broth with chicken bones, simmer 30 minutes or longer (longer means more flavor)
3. Using a slotted spoon and a scoop with fine holes, remove and discard chicken from broth
4. Bring broth to boil and add remaining **Ingredients**
5. After 10 minutes, enjoy!
