20 minutes preparation, 20 minutes cooking time

**Description**

A happy accident - I meant to make Shepard Pie with leftover mashed potatoes, but turns out I didn’t have ground beef. This recipe was the result!

**Ingredients**

* 3 cups of leftover mashed potatoes (cooled)
* ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
* ½ tsp Seasoned salt
* ½ tsp black pepper
* ¼ cup melted butter
* ½ cup seasoned breadcrumbs

**Directions**

1. Preheat oven to 450 F and spray a foil lined baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray
2. In a large bowl, mix together potatoes, cheese, seasoned salt and pepper
3. Lay out your station - potatoes then butter in a bowl, breading on a plate and baking tray
4. One at a time, roll a ball of potatoes, place in butter then breading and then on pan
5. Bake for 20 minutes, flipping once half way

**Notes**

*WHY BUTTER?* I ran out of eggs

*ALTERNATIVE*: Add cheese to the center of potatoes for an even nicer treat 

*PRO-TIP*: Don't mash the potatoes into oblivion, like I did. It makes them flatten in the oven
