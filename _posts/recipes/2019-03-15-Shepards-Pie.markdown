**Ingredients**

* 1 lb ground beef
* 5 carrots, peeled and cut into large chunks
* ½ cup water
* 1 Tbs ketchup
* 2 Tbs all purpose flour
* ¾ cup beef broth
* Leftover mashed potatoes (4 cups works well)
* ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

**Directions**

1. Preheat oven to 350 F and spray a 9 x 13" baking pan with nonstick cooking spray
2. Brown ground beef in skillet until no longer pink
3. Meanwhile, combine carrots and water in microwaveable dish with lid. Cover and microwave for 6 minutes on HIGH or until easily mashed with a fork.
4. Drain ground beef
5. Add ketchup, flour and beef broth to beef and simmer for 1 minute, pour into pan as base layer
6. Mash carrots and spread over beef
7. Add cheese to potatoes and microwave for 2 minutes on HIGH to soften and make it easier to spread
8. Cover carrot layer with potatoes
9. Bake for 25 minutes

**Notes**

*PRO-TIP*: To freeze this, assemble in pan and let cool, then cover with cling film and tin foil. Lasts up to 4 weeks.

*ALTERNATIVE*: Add 1 tsp seasoning salt and 1 Tbs black pepper to potatoes for more flavor.

*ONION-FRIENDLY*: Add ½ minced onions to beef in step 1
