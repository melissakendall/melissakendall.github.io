**Description**

5 minutes preparation, 40 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

* 1.5 lbs stewing beef cut into thin strips
* 2 tsp salt
* ½ tsp black pepper
* ½ tsp garlic powder
* 1 Tbs flour
* 4 Tbs olive oil
* 1 can beef broth
* 1 can cream of mushroom soup
* 1 cup sour cream
* Cooked egg noodles

**Directions**

1. Sprinkle salt, black pepper and garlic powder on beef then flour
2. In a large skillet, quickly brown meat in oil
3. Add beef broth and mushroom soup
4. Simmer on low for 30 minutes, covered
5. Stir in sour cream right before serving over cooked egg noodles

**Notes**

*ONION FRIENDLY*: Original recipe includes mushrooms and onions added in step 3

*CHOICE OF MEAT*: I use stewing beef because it is very cheap and I can’t really tell the difference after an hour of boiling. Use a higher quality cut if desired
