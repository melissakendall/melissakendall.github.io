**Description**

10 minutes preparation, 25 minutes cooking time, 1 - 8+ hours marinating time

**Ingredients**

* 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
* 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
* ¼ cup plus 2 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
* 1 Tbs cumin
* 4 tsp paprika
* 2 tsp turmeric
* 1 tsp salt
* ½ tsp garlic salt
* ½ tsp cinnamon
* ½ tsp black pepper
* ¼ tsp cayenne

**Directions**

1. Slice chicken into large pieces and place in large zip lock bag
2. Add spices, ¼ cup olive oil, 1 tsp salt and ½ tsp black pepper to bag. 
3. Shake bag to coat and marinate for 1 hour at least, or overnight.
4. Preheat oven to 400 F. Line baking sheet with tin foil and spray with nonstick cooking oil. Spread chicken pieces evenly on pan.
5. Cook for 15 minutes, turning at the 8 minute mark.
6. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing meat into small pieces
7. In a large pan, heat 2 Tbs oil and sautee chicken until smallest pieces are crispy
9. Serve warm in pita’s with tahini and humus! 

**Notes**

*PRO TIP*: The final steps of crisping the chicken are essential! I tried the chicken before and after crisping - a huge difference.

*ALTERNATIVE*: I have not tried this, but to make it easier it might work to broil the chicken at 500 F in the oven for 5-6 minutes after cooking to crisp up. 

*EASY*: Also have not tried this, but I suspect it would work well. Place all **Ingredients** in slow cooker for 4 hours on HIGH. Saute as descried in steps 7 & 8.
