15 minutes preparation, 2 hours 10 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

* 2 lbs stewing beef cut into 1" chunks
* 1 tsp garlic salt
* ½ tsp black pepper
* 3 tbs vegetable oil
* 3 cups water
* 4 tsp beef base (or beef bouillon - I use Costco beef base)
* 1 cup red wine (Merlot is great)
* 1 tsp dried parsley
* 3 red potatoes cut into 1" chunks
* 3 carrots cut into 1" chunks
* 3 tbs corn starch
* 3 tbs water

**Directions**

1. Season beef with garlic salt and pepper, then brown quickly on high heat with oil in large dutch oven or pot (The beef will stick to the bottom and make a mess - this is expected!)
2. Add beef base, water, wine and parsley to pot and stir
3. Bring to boil then cover and simmer on low heat for 1 hour
4. Add potatoes and carrots to pot, stir
5. Combine corn starch and water and add to pot, stir
6. Simmer 1 hour, serve
