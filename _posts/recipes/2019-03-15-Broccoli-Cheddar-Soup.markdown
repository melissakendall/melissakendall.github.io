10 minutes preparation, 20 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

* ¼ cup butter
* ¼ cup flour
* 2 cups half-and-half
* 2 cups chicken stock
* ½ lb broccoli, cut into small pieces
* ¼ tsp nutmeg
* 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese 
* ½ tsp garlic powder
* Salt and pepper, to taste

**Directions**

1. Melt butter in saucepan and whisk in flour. Let brown for 3-4 minutes
2. Whisk in half-and-half and chicken stock
3. Add broccoli and simmer for 6-8 minutes or until broccoli has reached desired doneness
4. Add remaining **Ingredients** and stir to melt cheese

**Notes**

*PRO TIP*: I used marble cheese, turned out quite good

*ONION-FRIENDLY*: Add ½ cup chopped onions to butter in step 1 and sautee until browned, before adding flour

*ONION-LIGHT*: Add ½ tsp onion-powder or add onion in tea-ball for a lighter onion flavor

*PRO-TIP*: The size of the broccoli pieces will have a direct impact on how long the soup takes to cook (step 3)
