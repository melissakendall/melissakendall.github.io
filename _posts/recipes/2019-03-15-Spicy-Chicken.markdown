**Description**

Very spicy

5 minutes preparation, 3 hours cooking time

**Ingredients**

* 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
* 1 package dry ranch dressing 
* 1 cup sriracha sauce

**Directions**

1. Place chicken in slow cooker
2. Combine dressing and sriracha in bowl
3. Pour over chicken
4. Cook on HIGH for 4 hours
5. Pull chicken with forks and serve on buns with cheese
