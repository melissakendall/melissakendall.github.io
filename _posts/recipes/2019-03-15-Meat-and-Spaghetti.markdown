60 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

*Spaghetti Balls*

* 8 oz uncooked spaghetti noodles
* 2 eggs
* ½ cup flour
* 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
* ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
* ½ cup sour cream 

*Meat Sauce*

* 2 lbs ground beef 
* 2 cans tomato sauce (398mL/13.5oz)
* 1 can tomato paste (156mL/5.5oz)
* 1 tbs white sugar
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp dried basil
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* ½ tsp Italian seasoning
* ½ tsp ground black pepper
* ½ tsp onion powder
* ½ tsp garlic salt

**Directions**

*Spaghetti Balls* 

1. Cook noodles for recommended time (usually 10 minutes)
2. Preheat oven to 350 F and line a large baking pan with foil, grease pan generously
3. Drain and rinse noodles and place in large bowl
4. Add all other **Ingredients** and mix with hands. You want to get the noodles cut up into 1-2" pieces, so mix aggressively!
5. One by one, scoop small balls of spaghetti into your hand and form into lumps, place on cookie sheet. The mixture will be very sticky so you may want to grease your hands. Aim for ~2" balls.
6. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown
7. Serve with meat sauce (or 2 lbs of cooked ground beef in spaghetti sauce)

*Meat Sauce* 

1. Brown ground beef and drain liquid
2. Add all other **Ingredients** and bring to a boil
3. Lower temperature and simmer for 30 minutes
