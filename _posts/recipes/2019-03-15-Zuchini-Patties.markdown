**Description**

Picture shows fried version.

10 minutes preparation, 20 minutes cooking time

**Ingredients**

* 2 cups grated zucchini
* 2 eggs
* ¼ cup chopped onion
* ½ cup all-purpose flour
* ½ cup Parmesean cheese
* ½ cup shredded marble cheese 
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* ½ tsp salt
* ¼ tsp pepper

**Directions**

1. Preheat oven to 350 F and grease a large baking pan
2. In a medium bowl, combine all **Ingredients**. 
3. Using a large spoon or ice cream scoop, spoon mixture in balls onto sheet
4. Flatten balls with hand or a spatula
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until golden brown, flipping half way through

**Notes**

*ONION FREE*: Omit, or to retain taste: pulverize onions in food processor, or use 2 tsp onion powder

*PRO TIP*: To flatten and ensure a crispier patty, spray the back of a flat utensil with nonstick cooking spray to flatten.

*ALTERNATIVE*: I actually used marble cheese because we ran out of cheddar, but lots of other types of cheese would work well!
