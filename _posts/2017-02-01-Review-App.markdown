I have been frustrated recently when I go to restaurants and can't remember what I liked the last time I visited. Sure, I could post this on any number of review websites, but what if I dont want anyone knowing my opinion?

Such a solution may exist, but I was not able to find one. So, I made my own.

It also doubles as a place to store your personal inventory of books, games, etc. 

[Check it out](http://melissakendall.ca/razor-reviews/public/#/login).
