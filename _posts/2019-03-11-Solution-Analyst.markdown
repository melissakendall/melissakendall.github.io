My first book, Marked for Harvest, has been submitted to publishers. Marked for Harvest is a paranormal romance short adult novel. Given that this is the first time I've submitted anything, it will almost certainly be rejected. I look forward to receiving my first ever rejection!

While I wait, I'm learning more about self-editing, and working on the outline for the second book in the Harvest series.
