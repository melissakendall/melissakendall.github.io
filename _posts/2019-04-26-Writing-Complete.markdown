Camp Nano is complete and so is my second manuscipt. This one is shorter at only 40,000 pages - I may decide to double the length by adding another 40k words next Camp Nano, which should be around July.

This book started as a romantic suspense, but as with the last one has evolved to include paranormal elements.

I think it happens because I am not a huge fan of romance novels that don't include some kind of magic or fantasy. 

My first manuscript is going through a fourth round of editing - mostly surface edits. The beginning is rough, but the later chapters are much more solid.
